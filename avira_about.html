Primary Goal

i.) To inculcate life changing values like integrity self-control, courtesy and indomitable spirit.
ii.) To build strength – Both in body and character. To increase confidence – Both in physical ability and personal potential. To build flexibility – Both on mat and in life.
iii.) Helping each student one-on-one to achieve personal physical and mental health goals.
iv.) Helping members to manage stress and increase mental and physical strength.

Description

SHORT TERM GOALS
 i.) Encouraging and socializing with students in order to teach them the importance of martial arts.

ii.) Ensuring regular practice sessions.


MID - TERM GOALS
i.) To focus on sports specific training i.e. building stamina, flexibility, strength and speed.
ii.) Organizing events like Nirbhaya, Moksha, Chakravyuh etc. in order to bring amazing experience for the students.


LONG TERM GOALS
i.) To master both mind and emotions by providing students with different challenges during practice sessions.
ii.) To not learn martial arts for the sole purpose of Self-Defence but also to learn discipline, focus and respect.
