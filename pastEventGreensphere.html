Past Events

Bioheritage Yatra

To celebrate Earth Day, students embarked on a Bioheritage Yatra, exploring the diverse biodiversity of Amrit Udyan. This enriching experience allowed participants to witness firsthand the variety of plant and animal life within the garden, fostering a deeper appreciation for environmental conservation and the natural world. The excursion was designed to highlight the importance of preserving biodiversity and encouraged students to engage more actively in protecting our planet's ecosystems.

Plantation Drive

Students actively participated in a tree-planting initiative aimed at promoting greenery and environmental sustainability. This drive was organized to enhance the campus's green cover and contribute to the broader goal of environmental conservation. By planting trees, students not only helped improve the local ecosystem but also gained hands-on experience in sustainable practices. The event emphasized the importance of individual contributions to environmental health and encouraged ongoing commitment to ecological stewardship.

Cleanliness Drive

A campaign was organized to clean and beautify the local area, with the goal of fostering a cleaner and more sustainable environment. Students and community members came together to remove litter, tidy up public spaces, and enhance the overall appearance of the surroundings. This initiative aimed to promote environmental responsibility and raise awareness about the impact of cleanliness on public health and community well-being. Through their collective efforts, participants contributed to creating a more pleasant and hygienic environment for everyone.

Online Workshop Events

We hosted a series of online workshops featuring guest speakers from various locations who shared their expertise on environmental topics. These virtual sessions aimed to inspire and educate students about pressing environmental issues, innovative solutions, and sustainable practices. By bringing together experts from different fields, the workshops provided valuable insights and fostered a greater understanding of environmental stewardship among participants.
