Primary goal

Protégé IGDTUW is dedicated to fostering personal and professional growth among students through one-on-one mentorship.
The society aims to provide individualized attention to each mentee, ensuring they receive the guidance and support necessary for self-study and independent development.
By promoting a collaborative environment, Protégé IGDTUW helps both mentors and mentees thrive together, creating a nurturing space for learning and mutual growth.

Description

Protégé IGDTUW is a college society committed to empowering students by offering a structured mentorship program.
The society organizes cohorts where students work on projects over a set period, receiving personalized mentorship to enhance their skills and knowledge.
The one-to-one mentorship model ensures that each student gets the focused attention they need, allowing for tailored guidance and effective doubt resolution. In addition to mentorship, Protégé IGDTUW organizes sessions with professionals actively working in various fields, providing students with industry insights and real-world perspectives.
The society also collaborates with tech influencers, bringing in diverse expertise and inspiration to the student community. Protégé IGDTUW encourages self-study and independence, helping students build strong foundations for their future careers while fostering meaningful connections with their mentors and industry professionals.
In addition to its mentorship programs and cohorts, Protégé IGDTUW actively engages in research activities.
The society members conduct research on various topics, contributing to the academic and professional community. They share their findings and insights by writing and publishing blogs on Medium, making their research accessible and beneficial to a wider audience. This practice not only enhances the knowledge base of the society members but also establishes Protégé IGDTUW as a contributor to ongoing discussions in their fields of interest.
