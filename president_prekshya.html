Meet the President

Simmi Thapad

Simmi Thapad is the President of Prekshya, the official photography society of IGDTUW. She is a final-year student in the CSE class of 2025. Under her leadership, Prekshya aims to nurture the innate photographic talent in every individual. The society is dedicated to providing comprehensive experiences and opportunities, enabling IGDTUW photographers to elevate their skills and creativity to new heights.


1) Nominee Photographer, Kalakari Film Festival, India, 2023 & 2024

2) Photographer, Kalakari Magazine, India, 2022

3) Top Appreciable Photographers ranked under 200, Global Photography Contest, 2022
