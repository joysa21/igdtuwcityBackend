Meet the President

Ananya Gupta

Ananya Gupta, a B.Tech CSE student from IGDTUW's class of 2025, is the inspiring President of the AI Club. Known for her exceptional technical achievements, she has consistently demonstrated her expertise in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Ananya's leadership in the AI Club reflects her commitment to fostering innovation and empowering students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the field. Her passion for technology and her ability to translate complex concepts into practical applications make her a respected figure among her peers. Under her guidance, the AI Club continues to thrive as a hub of creativity and collaboration. Ananya Gupta is well known for the following achievements in the Tech-field :

1) SWE Intern'24 @Microsoft

2) HSF India Trainee'23 @CERN

3) NXP WIT'22 Scholar

4) JPMC Code For Good 2024 Finalist

5) First Runner Up position in the Deep Fake Detection Category at the Rajasthan Police Hackathon 1.0

6) 2nd place in the tea x pkgx hackathon

7) Top 5 in the Telangana AI Mission (T-AIM) Academic Grand Challenge for Climate Change
