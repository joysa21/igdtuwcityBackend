Instagram Knowledge Series

The Instagram Knowledge Series was a highlight for Lean In IGDTUW, offering detailed roadmaps for learning various technologies. The series was structured into three levels—Basics, Intermediate, and Advanced—to accommodate all skill levels, from beginners to those with more experience. Each roadmap was carefully designed to provide a clear and structured learning path, helping participants enhance their technological skills and knowledge.

Lean In Hacks 4.0

Lean In Hacks 4.0 The 4th edition of our annual hackathon, Lean In Hacks 4.0, was a tremendous success! This year, we had 5 judges evaluating the innovative projects, 8 mentors providing guidance to participants, and over 10 sponsors contributing to a prize pool of ₹1.85 Lakhs. With more than 1000 hackers joining us over the weekend, we witnessed an incredible array of creative and groundbreaking projects.

Lean In Annual Mentorship Cohort

The Lean In Annual Mentorship Cohort was a resounding success, featuring over 400 mentees and approximately 10 mentors. Participants were guided through 5 distinct mentorship circles, including Machine Learning, App and Web Development, and Data Structures and Algorithms (in C++ and Java). The cohort was meticulously planned, with regular sessions and structured practice work to ensure comprehensive learning and growth for all involved.
