Past Events

Bennett University: Euphoria 2023

Hypnotics participated in Euphoria, Bennett University's annual cultural festival. While they didn't place in the top three, their performance was well-received, and they gained valuable experience and exposure. This participation allowed the team to network with other dance enthusiasts and learn from different performances, enriching their own skills and creativity.

SGT University: Genesis

Hypnotics secured the second prize at Genesis, the cultural fest of SGT University. Their captivating performance in various dance forms like hip-hop and freestyle impressed both the judges and the audience, showcasing their creativity and dynamic stage presence. The team's energy and enthusiasm were palpable, earning them admiration and applause from the crowd.

Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT): Melange

At Melange, IIFT's cultural fest, Hypnotics clinched the third prize. Their dynamic routine, featuring krump and waacking, stood out among the competitors, earning them a well-deserved place on the podium. The innovative choreography and seamless transitions between dance styles highlighted their versatility and commitment to excellence.
