Photoshoot: Psychedelic Euphoria ZENA X PREKSHYA

Zena teamed up with Prekshya, the university's photography club, for a captivating photoshoot themed "Psychedelic Euphoria." This collaboration showcased the vibrant talents of Zena's models and Prekshya's photographers. The theme "Psychedelic Euphoria" centred on bold, vivid colours and surreal designs, evoking a sense of altered reality and intense visual pleasure. Zena's makeup artists created mesmerising looks with neon hues and intricate patterns, transforming the models into living art. Prekshya's photographers captured these striking visuals with skill, using creative lighting and angles to enhance the psychedelic effect. The result was a series of stunning images that perfectly embodied the theme's vibrant and surreal aesthetic.

Event: OOTD 2.0:Sustainably Styled Soiree ZENA X ROTARACT

Zena teamed up with the Rotaract society to organise a spectacular event titled "OOTD 2.0: Sustainably Styled Soiree”. This collaboration aimed to blend the passion for fashion with a commitment to sustainability, creating a unique and inspiring evening for everyone involved. The event showcased innovative approaches to fashion where style met conscience, emphasizing the importance of sustainable practices in the world of fashion.Thy also explored how to incorporate eco-friendly choices into their wardrobes without compromising on style Zena and the Rotaract society's joint effort not only sparked creativity but also raised awareness about sustainable fashion, making a significant impact within our college community.

ZEAL by Zena

Zeal by Zena, a sub-branch of Zena—the fashion society of IGDTUW—specialises in makeup artistry and body painting. This talented team excels at creating stunning makeup and body art, frequently showcased at university events and fairs. Their artists are renowned for their creativity, skill, and ability to transform individuals into living canvases. Beyond event stalls, the team is responsible for the makeup of Zena’s models at every event, ensuring they shine on the runway with flawless, cohesive looks. Passionate about fostering self-expression and confidence, Zeal by Zena continues to leave a lasting impact, making them an essential part of Zena and the IGDTUW community.
