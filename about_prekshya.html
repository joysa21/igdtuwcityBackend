Primary Goal

Prekshya, the official photography society of IGDTUW, believes that a good photographer lies in every single one of us. We aim to provide holistic experiences and opportunities to the photographers of IGDTUW, to help them take their skills and creativity to the next level!

Description

All year long, our society holds creative and collaborative events such as Photowalks with different societies and even different colleges, photography workshops, competitions, photoshoots etc. We also cover the major events and fests that take place in the University, creating digital memories of college life at IGDTUW.
As the President of Prekshya, Simmi Thapad warmly invites you to join the society, believing that every individual possesses a unique photographic potential waiting to be unleashed. With her extensive experience and passion for photography, Simmi is dedicated to creating an environment where members can explore and develop their skills in a supportive and inspiring community. By joining Prekshya, you will gain access to a plethora of opportunities, including hands-on workshops, photo walks, and collaborative projects that will not only enhance your technical abilities but also ignite your creative vision. Simmi's commitment to providing holistic experiences ensures that every member can grow and excel, making Prekshya the perfect platform for both budding photographers and seasoned enthusiasts to take their artistry to the next level.
