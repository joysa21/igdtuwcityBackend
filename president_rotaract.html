Khushi Goel

Khushi Goel, a 3rd year undergraduate pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at IGDTUW, is the esteemed President of the Rotaract Club at IGDTUW. Known for her kindness, helpful nature, and sweet demeanor, Khushi exemplifies leadership and compassion. Her dedication to the club and her fellow students is evident in her tireless efforts to foster a supportive and inclusive community. Under her guidance, the Rotaract Club has thrived, promoting various initiatives and events that embody the spirit of service and camaraderie. Khushi's remarkable ability to connect with others and her unwavering commitment to making a positive impact have made her an invaluable asset to the IGDTUW community.
