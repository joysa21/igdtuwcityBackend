Meet the President

Muskan Yadav

I am Muskan Yadav, a third-year B.Tech CSE student with a profound enthusiasm and passion for learning new technologies. My journey in the tech world has been driven by a curiosity to explore and understand the latest advancements, and I am always eager to expand my knowledge and skills. I am honored to serve as the President of Instinct, a life skills society dedicated to empowering individuals with essential skills for personal and professional growth. Instinct aims to create a supportive environment where members can develop critical life skills, ranging from communication and leadership to time management and emotional intelligence.

As the President of Instinct, I am committed to creating an inclusive and nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and empowered to reach their full potential. Together, we will embark on a journey of growth, learning, and transformation, equipping ourselves with the skills needed to thrive in today's dynamic world.
