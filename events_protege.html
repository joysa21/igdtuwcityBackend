Past Events

CODESOLSTICE

CODESOLSTICS is a specialized cohort organized by Protégé IGDTUW, focusing on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA). This cohort offers an immersive learning experience that includes collaborative problem-solving, one-to-one mentorship, real-world application and engaging coding challenges. Participants have the opportunity to work closely with mentors , apply their knowledge to practical scenarios, and enhance their problem-solving skills. We got more than 220+ registration for mentees and 50+ registration for mentor.

DEVSPRINT21

The DevSprint21 cohort organized by Protege IGDTUW is a 21-day intensive program designed for students to independently create a project. During this period, students are encouraged to learn on their own, but they have the opportunity to seek guidance from mentors whenever they encounter difficulties. for mentee.

PrepPro Contest

Protégé IGDTUW also organizes contests to foster a competitive spirit and enhance technical skills among students. This year, the society organized the PrepPro Contest, a Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) competition that saw significant participation from students. The contest provided a platform for students to showcase their problem-solving abilities and compete with their peers, furthering their understanding and proficiency in DSA concepts.
