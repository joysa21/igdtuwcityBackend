Primary goal

Zena motivates its members and provides them with a platform to showcase their talents and creativity on the stage. It is a society that is the most dynamic and promises the best opportunities for students. Zena is more than just a fashion society and provides a platform to amplify voices, challenge stereotypes, and foster a world that embraces individuality. In this session , Zena promoted the theme "Neurodiversity," which is the idea that embraces variations in the human brain. Conditions like autism, ADHD, and dyslexia are not disabilities but simply variations. These individuals have their own uniqueness and talents, which have to be embraced and accepted by society.

Description

Zena is the fashion society of IGDTUW where passion, creativity, and confidence meet the world of fashion and modeling. With its performances, Zena aims to boost the confidence of its members by highlighting social causes. Zena pushes boundaries by breaking the prejudices existing in the field of fashion. We support body positivity and select members without restrictions on height, weight, or body types. All one must have is the zeal and enthusiasm to be on the stage.
