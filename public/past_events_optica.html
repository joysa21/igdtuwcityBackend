COSMOHUNT

Our Annual Treasure Hunt Event :
The aim of the game was to find the final treasure, for which participants had to solve a crossword, which was based on space trivia and general physics questions. To get to the crosswords, the teams had to pick a random slip which would have a riddle to the location (in the university campus) and then look for an envelope hiding the QR to a pdf. This pdf was space and spacecraft themed, where the participants had to look for the link to the crossword.

TREK LUNARIS

The event began with Mr Parminder Singh giving insight on the various kinds of telescopes, their parts and how to operate them. The students were then taught on topics like different phases of the moon, the latitudes needed to use the reflective telescope, how to reduce error in the imaging process and the softwares needed to process the raw images. The students were then each given an opportunity to view the surface of the moon, jupiter and the rings of saturn through the telescope. The event concluded with a vote of thanks to the speaker and the lighting of lanterns. Everyone was provided with snacks and bookmarks.
