Past Events

MINUTES TO ME

Minutes to me: An event initiated as a safe space for participants to share their thoughts, opinions, personal stories or any thought provoking discussion for a more enriched sense of community. Afterall, putting the words out to the world is the first step into bringing a change.

MOMent

MOMent: A special event dedicated to our lifetime well wishers and best friends, our mothers. We believe in finding ways to thank them everyday but for this mother's day, Technoliterati hosted a platform to share with us a tapestry of most memorable MOMents with your mothers in any form be it a poem, passage or art

BOOK Swap

Book Swap: What better excuse to bring bookworms out of their shells than luring them with more books and company of fellow bookworms. A day filled with intriguing story sharings and lots of fun games. This is an event to keep on your radar.

STELLER

Steller: Story-teller or as we call shroud it ‘Stellar’ is one of TechnoLiterati’s main events where participants are allowed to let their creativity soar to the highest skies and earn the greatest title of a Stellar story teller.

Tête-à-tête

Tête-à-tête: An event hosted with Womxn in Cloud. An informative day filled with lots of cloud knowledge, super informative events and wholesome discussions.













AAROHAN

Aarohan: The literary fest of IGDTUW, a celebration of literature as an inseparable part of innovation and progress towards development. It hosts a plethora of events the highlight being ‘Mock United Nation (MUN)’ along with various writing, recitation and other fun-filled competitions and events.




TechnoClash

TechnoClash: A collaboration between TechnoLiterati and Innerve, the tech-fest of IGDTUW, offers an opportunity to indulge in debate on a myriad of topics. There’s only 1 rule: don’t hold back!
