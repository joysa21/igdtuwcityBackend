Primary goal

Minerva stands as the esteemed tech forum of IGDTUW, offering a conduit for seasoned professionals and alumni to impart their expertise in various technical spheres such as web development, AR/VR, competitive programming, automation, and robotics via engaging sessions and informative articles. Minerva seeks to cultivate a dynamic space where meaningful connections pave the way for endless possibilities.

Description

The alumni of IGDTUW share their experiences in attaining notable scholarships and recount their journeys to securing positions in renowned companies.

The vision of Minerva is to foster a culture of learning & collaboration and empower the tech community at IGDTUW.
