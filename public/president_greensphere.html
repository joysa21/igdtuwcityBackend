Meet the President

Mitali Rani

Mitali Rani is a dedicated third-year CSE student at IGDTUW and currently serves as the President of Green Sphere, the university's Environmental Society. With a passion for environmental conservation, Mitali has been at the forefront of numerous initiatives aimed at promoting ecological awareness and sustainable practices within the campus community.
Under her leadership, Green Sphere has successfully organized various activities such as sapling distribution drives, poster-making competitions, and plantation events. Mitali's commitment to fostering a culture of environmental responsibility is evident through her proactive approach and effective organization of events that engage and inspire her peers. Her work not only enhances environmental awareness but also encourages her fellow students to adopt greener practices in their daily lives.
Mitali’s leadership and enthusiasm for environmental causes reflect her dedication to making a positive impact both at IGDTUW and beyond.
