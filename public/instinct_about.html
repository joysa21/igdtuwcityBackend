Primary goal

The primary goal of Instinct is to create an environment which makes people feel included and heard.
To talk about myths and taboos of society and break them.

Description

At Instinct, we are committed to challenging societal myths and breaking taboos that often hinder personal growth and community development. We believe in the power of conversation and education to dismantle stereotypes and misconceptions, paving the way for a more inclusive and empathetic society.
Our society hosts a variety of events, workshops, and discussion forums that encourage participants to engage in meaningful conversations about pressing social issues. These activities are designed to:
Promote Inclusivity: We strive to ensure that every voice is heard and valued, fostering a sense of belonging and acceptance among all members. Break Taboos: We address and challenge societal myths and taboos, encouraging critical thinking and open-mindedness. Empower Individuals: We provide a platform for individuals to share their stories and experiences, empowering them to make a positive impact on their communities. Foster Education and Awareness: Through educational initiatives and awareness campaigns, we aim to inform and enlighten our members about various social issues and injustices. Inclusive Minds Society is more than just a community; it is a movement towards a more inclusive, understanding, and compassionate world. Join us in our mission to break barriers, challenge norms, and create a society where everyone feels valued and respected.
