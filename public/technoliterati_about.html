Primary goal

At TechnoLiterati, we believe in the power of diversity in thought and creativity. Let your mind's gears turn as we indulge in a myriad of activities, ranging from hands-on tech skills development sessions to book exchange events. Our mission is to foster a community that pushes the boundaries of interdisciplinary learning and inspiration, exploring the unique intersections of technology and literature. Emphasis on networking and personal growth while building valuable relationships with like-minded peers is also one of our core values.

Description

TechnoLiterati prides itself as the premier technical and literary society of IGDTUW.
We celebrate the convergence of two seemingly opposite yet equally creative forms of expression. This vision is only realized through the core team of our society i.e, Creative Team, Content Team, PR Team and EM Team who work tirelessly to achieve our goal.
