Past Events

SOW & GROW

Our plantation drive under Ecostellar, blossomed into a memorable affair! On Jan 23, we ventured to a community park where we planted 20 saplings. We engaged in lively games and delved into enriching discussions about sustainability.

Blood Donation Camp

The blood donation camp, in collaboration with "Blood Connect," surpassed 40 donors, 80 units of blood and 103+ individuals. We also hosted RCND President Rtn. Cdr. CP Sharma and Rtn. Vimal Gupta.

E-Waste Donation Drive

Under our sustainability umbrella project “Ecostellar”, we organized a five-day E-waste and Cloth collection drive. The drive collected 4.4kg of electronic waste which was handed over to Attero Pvt Ltd for responsible disposal.

Chapters, Charades & Chuckles: Aarohan

On 19th January 2024, we reminisced the unforgettable fusion of literature and entertainment at our Chapters, Charades, and Chuckles event in collaboration with IGDTUW’s literary fest Aarohan!
