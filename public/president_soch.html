Meet the President

Mahi Dhiman

Mahi Dhiman is a third-year CSE student and the passionate and visionary President of Soch, the creative society at IGDTUW. With her dynamic leadership, Mahi has transformed Soch into a thriving community that encourages students to think beyond the conventional boundaries of textbooks and embrace a more holistic and imaginative perspective of the world. Under Mahi's guidance, Soch has evolved into a nurturing platform where students can freely explore and express their creativity through various forms of art, including writing and painting. She has been instrumental in organizing workshops, competitions, and group sessions that provide students with the opportunity to hone their skills and showcase their talents. Mahi's vision for Soch extends beyond mere artistic expression; she aims to create an environment where students can discuss and share their ideas, thoughts, and experiences, fostering a sense of community and mutual inspiration.

We also know her for her fantastic achievements in the Tech-field. Some of them are mentioned below.

1) Research Intern @HCL Technologies

2) Graphic Designed & UI/UX Intern @ Godspeed Systems
