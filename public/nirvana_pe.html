Designathon 2024 by Nirvana

At Nirvana, The Design Club of IGDTUW, we recently conducted a successful Designathon in April 2024. This event featured two online competitions: a logo design competition and a music album redesign competition. Both competitions received an overwhelming number of participants and impressive submissions, showcasing the enthusiasm and talent of students from various colleges. The event was conducted online, while the felicitation ceremony took place offline at our university, celebrating not just the winners but every participant and their outstanding contributions.
