Meet the President

Anshika Durgapal

Anshika Durgapal is currently a third-year Mechanical and Automation engineering student at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. Since her first year (November 2022), she has been an active member of the university's dance society, where she has grown immensely, learning about dance, teamwork, discipline, and leadership. This society is like a family where members dance, laugh, eat, and learn together.

1) Theme Winner in 2021 Rotaract's Got Talent: Anshika's talent and creativity shone through as she won the theme competition, showcasing her ability to captivate audiences with her dance performances.

2) Second Runner-Up in 2022 Rotaract's Got Talent 2.0: Building on her previous success, she secured the second runner-up position in the 2022 edition of Rotaract's Got Talent, demonstrating consistent excellence and improvement in her dance skills.

3)Participation in Several Dance Competitions: Anshika has actively participated in numerous dance competitions, continually pushing herself to perform better and learn new styles, enriching her dancing journey.
