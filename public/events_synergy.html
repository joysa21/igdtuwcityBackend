Sportech

Synergy proudly celebrated an impressive achievement at Sportech, held at IIT Delhi from March 7-9, 2024. Our women's basketball team delivered an outstanding performance, showcasing their skill and determination, and clinched the 3rd position. This significant accomplishment highlighted the dedication and hard work of our athletes. It was an event that celebrated the spirit and resilience of our team, making it a memorable and proud moment for all.

Agastya

Adding to our list of achievements, Synergy celebrated a sensational victory at Aagastya, held at NSUT on March 11-12, 2022. Our women's football team demonstrated exceptional talent, fierce determination, and seamless teamwork to secure the 1st position. This triumphant win not only showcased the team's remarkable skills but also added another proud milestone to Synergy's storied history. It was a momentous occasion that highlighted the dedication and hard work of our athletes, making it an event to remember.

Aahvan'23

Synergy, our dynamic sports society, has a proud moment to share from Aahvan'23. Held at DTU from April 6th to 8th, 2023, this prestigious event saw our women's volleyball team dominate the competition with exceptional skill and teamwork. Their relentless effort and sportsmanship earned them the 1st place trophy, bringing glory and pride to our college. It was a spectacular display of athleticism and unity, marking a significant achievement in Synergy's history.
