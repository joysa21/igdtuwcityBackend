Primary goal

Robolution, the heart of innovation in robotics! Our society is a vibrant, inclusive community where everyone is equal and encouraged to share their ideas freely. Robolution was once a renowned hub for groundbreaking events and achievements in robotics, and now we are reigniting that spirit with a renewed commitment to collaboration and creativity.
The aim of Robolution is to create a friendly and open community where everyone can share their ideas and work together to advance robotics. We strive to inspire innovation and teamwork, making robotics accessible and exciting for all members.
Here, every member's voice matters. Whether you're a seasoned robotics enthusiast or just beginning your journey, Robolution is the place to express your creativity, learn from others, and contribute to exciting projects. Our goal is to foster an environment where diverse perspectives come together to push the boundaries of what is possible.

Join us as we embark on this exciting journey to make Robolution even bigger and better than before. Together, we can achieve new heights and shape the future of robotics. Welcome to a community where your ideas can truly make a difference. Welcome to Robolution!

On April 19, 2024, Robolution hosted an engaging and informative session led by Pooja Bhati. The session provided a comprehensive introduction to the world of robotics, aimed at sparking curiosity and enthusiasm among participants. Due to limited awareness about the revival of Robolution, only 30-40 students attended the event. Despite the smaller audience, the session was a resounding success and marked another milestone in our journey to foster innovation and collaboration in robotics.
