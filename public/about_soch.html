Primary goal

For all the students of IGDTUW who want to escape the daily academic lifestyle. SOCH brings out a platform for them where they can continue their creative art and writing. We give them the opportunity to showcase their creative mindset by hosting their own workshops, getting competition opportunities to represent SOCH and IGDTUW and even participating to learn new creative forms.

Description

We aim at motivating students to think beyond books and see the world with creative eyes. We help everyone to identify the missing voids and fill it with the creative sides they have.

Soch provided a platform to all the people who find comfort in writing and painting their heart out. We conduct workshops, competitions and group sessions where people discuss their ideas of the world, art and life. It also provides a platform to all the poets and painters who want to learn, compete and grow.
