Primary goal

Enactus IGDTUW aims to empower young leaders to use innovation and entrepreneurial skills to create a sustainable world where everyone can thrive. The society focuses on enhancing financial literacy, promoting eco-innovation, upcycling waste, and dissolving menstrual taboos. By joining Enactus, students can gain hands-on experience, network with industry leaders, and unlock exclusive opportunities, ultimately turning their ideas into impactful actions.

Description

Enactus IGDTUW is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students to make a real difference while boosting their skills and having fun. The society provides a platform for students to brainstorm and execute projects that solve real-world problems. Enactus IGDTUW's initiatives are guided by the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and include: Project Greenbucks: Enhancing financial literacy among India's youth and communities through engaging workshops, courses, and tools like budget planners, promoting eco-innovation for a sustainable future. Project PrakriKala: Upcycling local waste to create innovative products, fostering socio-economic welfare. Project Dharini: Aiming to dissolve menstrual taboos and create a safe, inclusive space for all menstruators. Through these projects, Enactus IGDTUW is committed to making a lasting impact and fostering a better, more inclusive world. Joining Enactus offers students the chance to gain valuable experience, be part of a global community, and drive meaningful change.
