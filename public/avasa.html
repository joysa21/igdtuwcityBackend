Avasa - The College Band 👩‍🎤🎸🥁
Avasa is Tarannum’s band. It comprises of talented instrumentalists, ranging from elegant violinists 🎻 to head-banging drummers 🥁. Avasa is where the cool kids are!
This is the club for part-time rockstars who live for beats and rhythms. Avasa competes in inter-college competitions amongst the best bands in the country. Not just in Delhi, but our band also participates in competitions hosted by universities outside of Delhi, like the IITs!
Join us if you’re an engineer by day, and rockstar by night, like our Avasa band-mates!
