Meet the President

Sohni Malik

Meet Sohni Malik, a pre-final year student pursuing Computer Science Engineering - Artificial Intelligence (CSE-AI) at IGDTUW (Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women). Sohni is a passionate learner and a naturally curious person who enjoys exploring a wide range of interests and trying out new things. Whether it's diving into the latest technology trends, experimenting with different hobbies, or embracing new challenges, she is always up for the adventure. She believes that the best way to learn and grow is by stepping out of her comfort zone. We also know her for her amazing contributions in so many fields, some of her achievements are mentioned below:

1) Multitalented and Versatile: Sohni has demonstrated her versatility by excelling in various domains, showcasing her ability to adapt and thrive in diverse environments.

2)Assistant trainer: Started as a trainee at Avira, dedicating herself to mastering the fundamentals and techniques of MMA. Promoted to Assistant Trainer for her dedication and acquired skills. Guided fellow enthusiasts on their martial arts journey, imparting knowledge and shaping training experiences.

3)Creative Team Head Coordinator at Innerve: Worked as the Creative Team Head Coordinator for Innerve, the annual technical fest of IGDTUW. Created various promotional materials like posters, brochures, and social media posts to ensure engaging visuals. Collaborated with the online team to develop and execute creative ideas to enhance the fest's online presence and success.

4)Leadership Qualities: Her leadership skills are evident through her roles and responsibilities, where she has consistently taken the initiative and led teams to success.
