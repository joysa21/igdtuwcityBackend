Meet the President

Aditi Goswami

Aditi Goswami, the dynamic President of Avira IGDTUW, leads the martial arts and parkour society with remarkable energy and dedication. As a 3rd year senior from the CSE-AI branch, she exemplifies a perfect blend of academic excellence and physical prowess. Known for her leadership skills and commitment to promoting fitness and discipline, Aditi has been instrumental in organizing various workshops and events that inspire students to embrace the values of martial arts and parkour. Her peers admire her for her unwavering determination, strategic thinking, and ability to motivate and guide others towards achieving their full potential.

Joining Avira IGDTUW is more than just becoming part of a society; it's about embracing a lifestyle of discipline, strength, and resilience. As the President, I can assure you that our society offers unparalleled opportunities for personal growth and self-improvement. Whether you’re a beginner or have prior experience in martial arts and parkour, Avira welcomes everyone with open arms. Our training sessions are designed to challenge you physically and mentally, helping you develop skills that extend beyond the practice mat. You’ll learn self-defense techniques, improve your agility and coordination, and build a strong foundation of physical fitness. But more than that, Avira fosters a supportive community where you can forge lasting friendships, learn the importance of teamwork, and cultivate a sense of camaraderie. We also focus on character development, teaching values such as discipline, perseverance, and respect. These qualities not only enhance your martial arts and parkour skills but also translate into other areas of life, including academics and future career endeavors. By joining Avira, you’ll be part of a society that encourages continuous learning, self-mastery, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
